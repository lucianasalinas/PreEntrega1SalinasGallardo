Desafío: El Exámen Final

Te toca demostrar que dominás todo lo que aprendiste en el curso realizando este examen. Tene cuidado, una sola respuesta incorrecta te hará empezar de nuevo.

I ____ on my way to work:

was

were

____ she have to go to school today?:

Do

Does

What ___ your favorite movie?:

is

are

John is a director, ___ makes movies:

he

she

___ is your dream job?:

What

Who

I ____ to Canada this winter.:

went

want

What is your favorite thing to ___ on a weekend?:

do

does

***Nota para profes:
Por favor presione los siguientes botones para acceder al examen:
cancelar > ok > clic derecho > actualizar

Esta la clave de respuestas; tenga en cuenta que distingue entre mayúsculas y minúsculas.
was, Does, is, he, What, went, do
